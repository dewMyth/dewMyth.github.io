It's no secret that the United States' low unemployment rate at 3.7% as of June 2019, is contributing to labor shortage issues in the restaurant industry. For restauranteurs what's more troublesome is trying to fill up labor gaps. A lot of restauranteurs and managers combat this but there is hope and the answer lies in introducing digital ordering innovation to the business.

With a limited staff resource at the restaurant, restaurants can utilize their resources to the maximum, firstly by streamlining some of the day-to-day restaurant ordering process and making a worthwhile, yet a long term investment on these digital ordering technologies.

For restauranteurs to stay engaged with a digitally discerning dining public, it is crucial that restauranteurs develop flexible ordering options, with digital tools that give customers the ability to find, order, pay and view order status through their smartphones- Not just these off-store solutions but restauranteurs must also offer in-store digital capabilities without having their customers wait for wait-staff too long.
