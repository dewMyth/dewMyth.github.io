Applova Exhibits at TRANSACT 2020

Meet us @ Booth #923

TRANSACT, the world’s largest payments technology trade show is taking place from April 27th- 30th in Mandalay Bay, Las Vegas, and Applova is excited to announce its participation for the third consecutive year. This global event brings together over 3,000 attendees and over 200 exhibitors to explore new technologies, trends and business opportunities and is the one show focused solely on the business of payment technologies. TRANSACT is powered by the Electronic Transactions Association (ETA), the world's largest payments industry trade group and the premier trade association representing the payments technology and acquiring ecosystem.

Applova's participation in Transact 2020 not only provides opportunities for and partnerships with ISOs, MSPs, VARs, Payment Processing Providers, Investors and Banking Agents but to showcase our latest cutting edge technologies in Digital Ordering allowing visitors to experience the end-to-end digital ordering solutions for restaurants.

Applova's product suite includes Online Ordering, Mobile Order Ahead, Self-ordering Kiosk, Kitchen Display System and Public Announcement System, enabling restaurants with a digitally seamless, stress-free operation of online and in-store ordering.

Over the years, Applova has integrated with various leading partners in the payments industry, Point of Sales, Hardware and eCommerce, and is looking towards expansion in channel opportunities and strategic partnerships.

Come visit Applova at booth #923 at TRANSACT 2020 for extensive networking sessions and educational workshops, while experiencing a tour around our innovative products and discover opportunities with Applova's fully integrated turnkey solutions.
