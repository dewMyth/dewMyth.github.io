Beat the kitchen's heat with Kitchen Display Systems

Tech | 3 Min Read

Let’s face it, the heat of the kitchen can get hot, and we’re not just talking about the cooking. During peak dining, your kitchen staff is working super hard to track, prioritize, and prepare customer orders. You want an exceptional dining experience for each customer, and that includes eliminating unnecessary order delays and errors. When kitchen operations breakdown, your staff gets stressed and your customer’s dining experience can be significantly impacted which is not only embarrassing… but can be a drag on your restaurant’s success.

We all hear about the digital transformation and how it continues to shape just about every aspect of our lives. And, while all industries have adopted new technologies to drive innovation – not all technology is created equal. Some technical solutions provide “nice to have” benefits, while other solutions are just down right critical to staying competitive in one’s industry.

For example, in the restaurant industry, nothing keeps the rhythm in motion more than smooth kitchen operations. The heat should be on the burners, not between kitchen staff when problems occur preparing orders. You want items cooked to order with the correct side dishes – the first time. Special requests cannot get lost in the shuffle between the order taker and the preparer. When kitchen problems occur during peak dining, they can cascade into bigger problems… and before you know it you have one large mess on your hands.

Here’s the good news! With the latest Kitchen Display Systems (KDS), you can avoid the unnecessary chaos in your kitchen and maintain the rhythm that is so critical to a restaurant’s success. These affordable fully integrated systems will eliminate headaches for your team while increasing smiles for your customers.

  • 70% reduction in customer complaints after installing Kitchen Display System (major restaurant chain).

Customer orders are immediately fired directly to the Kitchen Display System, simplifying the ordering process by eliminating manual processes and printed tickets. Some restaurants have reported a 10-15% increase in sales during peak dining just by having a POS system that can handle faster order transfer to the kitchen.

With the KDS, all kitchen staff have clear visibility to the order status, including the priority queue, special orders, and delayed orders. This added clarity can significantly improve your kitchen efficiency while providing seamless order management for your team.

The Kitchen Display System supports complex menus, and is customizable to your menu with color-coding and order run-times. Simple order navigation is included with convenient tap and swipe features.

These systems come with easy to mount state-of-the-art large screen displays, that can be installed anywhere in your kitchen without losing visibility.
