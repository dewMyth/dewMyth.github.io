About the Role

We are looking for someone with the passion for sales to join the team of this exciting new company to help grow it with our established channel partners along with cultivating new outlets. The ideal candidate will be self-driven and thrive on helping our partners succeed through driving pull through sales, facilitating the onboarding process, and providing market feedback to our development team for further enhancements to the solution.

As the solution is also very flexible, you should also have the ability to look at different business models and have the ability to work with our development team to configure the solution to match the need.

  • Close new sales and generate new revenue by adding new merchants

  • As an employee, meet and exceed defined weekly/monthly sales quotas

  • Deliver sales presentations to prospective channel partners
  • Use current experience and contacts for networking and expansion
  • Assist with merchant implementation, setup, and training
  • Maintain productive relationships with merchant base
  • Work as a team with managers, support coordinators, and customer service
