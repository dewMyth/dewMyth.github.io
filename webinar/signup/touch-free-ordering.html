Explore the New Touch-free Guest Experience for Your Restaurant

Date and Time:
June 18, 2020 | 2PM ET | 11AM PT

The common touchscreens and shared menu cards, along with the handling of money and card transactions, have presented a chance to spread germs.

While people return to their everyday experiences, dining out is still part of the picture, but people desire safety. Touch-free dining is one way for restaurant operators to provide security while opening doors to an enjoyable new normal living. It is designed to remove the need for customer touchpoints, keeping restaurant staff and guests at ease.

In this webinar we will cover:

  • Introduction to touch-free ordering

  • Why touch-free dining is important

  • How to enable touch-free dining cost-effectively

  • How adapting to touch-free ordering helps in making profits
